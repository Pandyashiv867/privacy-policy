Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the VixyraPDF mobile application. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. Data Collection & Processing

VixyraPDF is designed with a privacy-first approach. All document scanning, image processing, and PDF generation processes happen entirely locally on your device. We do not upload, process, or store your documents on our servers.

2. Permissions Requested

To provide our core services, we request the following permissions:

  • Camera: Required exclusively to scan documents and ID cards in real-time.
  • Storage: Required to save generated PDFs to your local device storage.

3. Google Drive Integration (Cloud Sync)

If you explicitly choose to sign in with your Google Account to use the Cloud Sync feature, we use the Google Drive API to backup and sync your PDFs to a private, app-specific folder in your own Google Drive. We cannot see, access, modify, or share your Google Drive files with any third parties.

4. Bug Reports

If you choose to submit a bug report through the app, the information you provide (name, email, description, and attached screenshot) is securely transmitted directly to our developer team to help improve the app.
